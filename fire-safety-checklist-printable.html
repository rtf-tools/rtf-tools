Critical: Never use ABC powder on a cooking oil fire — it spreads flames. Use a fire blanket. Never use water on electrical fires — use CO₂ only. Know your MCB location before a fire, not during one.

8 checks
Smoke detector in each bedroom, tested this month Critical

Charged phone accessible at night for 101 / 112 High
